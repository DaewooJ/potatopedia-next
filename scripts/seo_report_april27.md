## Recommendations

### 1. Wave 4 — high-priority new answer pages (next 2 weeks)

Priority order is based on (a) PAA frequency, (b) commercial/consumer search intent, and (c) data we already have on hand from existing ingests.

1. **What potatoes does McDonald's use?** — appears in 2 PAA threads, ties to Russet Burbank ingest. High brand-search volume.
2. **Where do Russet Burbank potatoes come from?** — Russet Burbank ingest gives us authoritative answer.
3. **Do potatoes cause blood sugar spikes?** — diet/health is the largest PAA cluster (15 questions). Lead with this.
4. **What are common potato growing mistakes?** — listicle format, high search volume.
5. **How many potatoes per 5-gallon bucket?** — long-tail consumer query, container-growing trend.
6. **What is a Kufri potato variety?** — India-market query, we have CPRI ingest data.
7. **What is the unhealthiest chip to eat?** — high-CTR question format, ties to processing content.
8. **Why were potatoes illegal in France?** — historical curiosity; high engagement potential; pairs with `irish-potato-famine`.
9. **How to calculate potato yield / 100 ft of row?** — strong farmer-intent, ties to `seed-potato-yield-calculator`.
10. **Can a diabetic have french fries / fried potatoes / baked potatoes?** — bundle these into one page or a tightly cross-linked cluster.

### 2. Optimize existing pages where we're close to ranking

Currently no page is ranked, so there is no "close to page 1" cohort yet. Once any URL surfaces in the top 100 in a future run, this section will list the on-page tweaks (title, H1, schema, internal links) most likely to push it onto page 1.

### 3. Quick wins — weak-competitor SERPs

These keywords are dominated by **thin auto-generated tables** rather than serious editorial content. Realistic medium-term displacement targets (3–6 months):

- `potato production by country` — competitors: worldpopulationreview, atlasbig (both thin tables). Our `/library` and country pages are richer.
- `potato consumption per capita` — competitors: helgilibrary (thin). Our country profiles + a dedicated answer page should outrank.
- `top potato producing countries` — competitors: worldpopulationreview, atlasbig.
- `potato yield per hectare by country` — no strong incumbent.
- `potato production by country 2024` — freshness signal favors us.

For each of these, the optimization path is: (a) fresh year in the title, (b) explicit numeric data table, (c) FAQ schema with the 3-5 most-asked PAA questions, (d) internal links from the country index page.

### 4. Don't expect rankings yet

It has been **3 weeks since Wave 1-3 deployment**. New domains require 3–6 months for Google to establish topical authority. The next meaningful re-run is **2026-05-27 to 2026-06-15** (4–7 weeks out). Earlier re-runs will likely show the same 0/50 — burning credits for no signal.
