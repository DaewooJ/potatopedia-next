# Potatopedia Blog — Forward Content Plan

**Audit date:** 2026-04-27
**Current inventory:** 15 published posts (1,879–2,965 words each), 8 categories, 9 countries covered

## Editorial positioning (going forward)

- **/blog** = stories, country case studies, market trends, news commentary, opinion-driven analysis. Timely. Narrative voice. Drives SEO freshness signals.
- **/knowledge** = evergreen reference articles. Encyclopedic. FAQ-driven. Citation-heavy. Structured tables.

A blog post should answer "what changed, what's the story, why does it matter now." A knowledge article should answer "what is this, what are the facts, with citations."

## Content strategy gaps identified

1. **Africa beyond Egypt** — 1.4 billion people, fastest-growing potato region (CIP data shows 5–8% annual growth in East Africa)
2. **Southeast Asia** — Bangladesh (8th producer), Indonesia, Vietnam, Philippines all underrepresented
3. **Latin America** — Peru is the cradle of potato but only mentioned in passing; Argentina, Colombia, Brazil all have notable industries
4. **Russia / Ukraine / Central Asia** — major producers, geopolitically significant, zero coverage
5. **News-driven posts** — climate adaptation, trade-policy shifts, processor M&A, listings, IPO commentary
6. **Comparative analyses** — country head-to-heads, processor head-to-heads
7. **Profiles of people / institutions** — CIP gene bank, Wageningen, ICAR-CPRI, individual breeders

## Editorial cadence recommendation

- **Target 1–2 posts per month** — keeps freshness signal alive without diluting quality
- **Mix Tier 1 + Tier 2** alternately — global geographic reach + topical hooks
- **Quarterly market commentary** — short 1,200-word "what just happened" pieces to maintain SEO freshness for "global potato market 2026" type queries
- **Refresh top performers annually** — keep the top 3 traffic earners updated with current-year data

## Internal-linking strategy (apply to existing + future posts)

Each blog post should:
1. Link to the related country profile in the first 3 paragraphs (e.g., Kenya post → /country/kenya)
2. Link to 2–3 evergreen /knowledge articles where the topic intersects
3. Include a "Related stories" section at the end with 3 other blog posts
4. End with a cross-link to /ask for users wanting to dig deeper

This was identified as a deficit in the audit (most existing posts don't link out aggressively). Recommend retroactive update across the existing 15 posts in a future batch.

## What NOT to write

- Generic listicles ("10 facts about potatoes") — competes with food-blog content, low authority signal
- "Best potato varieties for [X]" — already covered by /knowledge/potato-varieties-guide and /knowledge/kufri-potato-varieties-india
- Pure recipe content — out of scope; we're a knowledge platform, not a food blog
- Anything that duplicates an existing /knowledge article's scope — see audit (12 of 15 current posts overlap; don't add to that)
