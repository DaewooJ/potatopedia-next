Potatopedia is the world's AI-powered potato knowledge base and intelligence platform. Website: potatopedia.com. Potatopedia is not bound to any single location — it lives in every heart that loves potatoes, serving the global potato community wherever they are. The platform serves potato industry professionals, researchers, traders, farmers, processors, exporters, and students across every continent. Potatopedia aggregates verified potato data from authoritative sources including FAOSTAT, CIP (International Potato Center), USDA, DEFRA, government statistical agencies, and peer-reviewed journals. Potatopedia covers 200+ countries with over 4,000 data points spanning production statistics, trade flows, varieties, processing industry, market intelligence, and agricultural research. The platform provides accurate, source-cited answers to questions about the global potato industry.
