Agrico Potato Varieties — Official Breeder Data
Source: Agrico U.A. (Netherlands), www.agricopotatoes.com
Agrico is a Dutch cooperative of 1,300+ growers, breeding and marketing potato varieties for retail, traditional, fries, crisps, starch, and organic markets.

Variety: Fontane
Breeder: Agrico (Netherlands)
Maturity: Medium early to medium late
Skin Color: Yellow
Flesh Color: Light yellow
Tuber Shape: Long oval
Cooking Type: B
Primary Use: French fries, processing
Dry Matter: High
Yield: High
Disease Resistance: Good general resistance
Storage: Good storability
Special Features: Leading french fry variety across Europe and export markets. Excellent frying quality with consistent color and texture. Widely grown in the EU Potato Belt (Belgium, France, Netherlands).

Variety: Carolus
Breeder: Agrico (Netherlands)
Maturity: Medium late
Skin Color: Red
Flesh Color: Yellow
Tuber Shape: Round oval
Cooking Type: B-C
Primary Use: Table, multi-purpose (organic suitability)
Dry Matter: Medium-high
Yield: Good
Disease Resistance: Outstanding late blight resistance (both foliage and tuber)
Storage: Good
Special Features: Bred specifically for organic and low-input farming. One of the best late blight resistant table varieties available. Ideal for growers who want to reduce fungicide use.
