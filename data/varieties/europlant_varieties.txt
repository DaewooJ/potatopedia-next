Europlant Potato Varieties — Official Breeder Data
Source: Europlant Pflanzenzucht GmbH (Germany, est. 1992), www.europlant.biz
Europlant breeds and markets over 100 potato varieties, exporting to 70+ countries worldwide.

Variety: Bellarosa
Breeder: Europlant (Germany)
Maturity: Very early to early
Skin Color: Red
Flesh Color: Light yellow to yellow
Tuber Shape: Oval
Cooking Type: B (mainly firm cooking)
Primary Use: Table variety
Dry Matter: Medium
Yield: High early yield with large tuber sizes
Disease Resistance: PCN Ro1, Ro4. Potato wart disease type 1. Common scab: low. Bruising: low-medium.
Storage: Good dormancy
Special Features: Robust variety with high drought tolerance. Achieves very large, oval tubers with stable internal quality at an early stage. Rapid youth development.

Variety: Agria
Breeder: Europlant (Germany)
Maturity: Medium early
Skin Color: Yellow (netted)
Flesh Color: Yellow
Tuber Shape: Long oval
Cooking Type: B
Primary Use: Multi-purpose — French fries, crisps, granule and convenience products
Dry Matter: High
Yield: Very high, uniform large-sized tuber grading
Disease Resistance: PCN Ro1. Potato wart disease type 1. Common scab: medium-high. Bruising: very low to low.
Storage: Very dormant, suitable for long-term storage. Stable frying quality after storage.
Special Features: Good taste, low discolouration after cooking. One of the world's premier french fry varieties, widely grown across Europe, Middle East, and Central Asia.

Variety: Belana
Breeder: Europlant (Germany)
Maturity: Early to medium early
Skin Color: Yellow (smooth to netted)
Flesh Color: Yellow
Tuber Shape: Oval
Cooking Type: A-B (firm cooking)
Primary Use: Premium salad potato
Dry Matter: Medium
Yield: Good, uniform grading with high marketable yield
Disease Resistance: PCN Ro1, Ro4. Common scab: low. Spraing: low. Bruising: low.
Storage: Very dormant, suitable for long-term storage
Special Features: Most popular salad variety in Germany. Stable firm cooking quality with appealing yellow flesh, no discolouration after cooking. Tasteful year-round.

Variety: Glorietta
Breeder: Europlant (Germany)
Maturity: Very early to early
Skin Color: Yellow (smooth)
Flesh Color: Deep yellow
Tuber Shape: Long oval (salad shape)
Cooking Type: A (firm cooking)
Primary Use: Premium firm cooking ware potato
Dry Matter: Low-medium
Yield: Medium yield with high amount of packing sizes
Disease Resistance: PCN Ro1 (9), Ro4 (9). Potato wart disease type 1. Common scab: low. Bruising: low. Mechanical damage: low.
Storage: Good dormancy
Special Features: Smooth skin, very shallow eyes, excellent potato flavour. Premium retail variety for salad and boiling.

Variety: Laura
Breeder: Europlant (Germany)
Maturity: Medium early
Skin Color: Red
Flesh Color: Deep yellow
Tuber Shape: Oval to long oval
Cooking Type: B (mainly firm cooking)
Primary Use: Consumption/ware potato
Dry Matter: Medium-high
Yield: Medium to high tuber yield with uniform marketable yields
Disease Resistance: PCN Ro1, Ro2/3, Ro4, Ro5. Potato wart disease type 1. Common scab: low-medium. Bruising: low.
Storage: Good dormancy, classic storage variety
Special Features: Known as the "queen of red skin" — aromatic taste, attractive deep yellow flesh, good storability. Sensitive to Metribuzin herbicide.

Tuber Shape: Oval
