HZPC Potato Varieties — Official Breeder Data
Source: HZPC Holland B.V. (Netherlands, est. 1898), www.hzpc.com
HZPC is the world's largest seed potato company, breeding and marketing over 50 varieties worldwide.

Variety: Innovator
Breeder: HZPC (Netherlands)
Maturity: Medium early
Skin Color: White/light yellow
Flesh Color: Light yellow
Tuber Shape: Long oval
Cooking Type: B-C
Primary Use: French fries (QSR — Quick Service Restaurant)
Dry Matter: High
Yield: High
Disease Resistance: PCN resistant (Pa2, Pa3)
Storage: Good dormancy
Special Features: Big tubers, consistent high frying quality, industry standard for QSR french fries. One of the most widely grown processing varieties globally.

Variety: Colomba
Breeder: HZPC (Netherlands)
Maturity: Very early
Skin Color: Yellow, very attractive bright skin
Flesh Color: Light yellow
Tuber Shape: Oval
Cooking Type: A-B (firm cooking)
Primary Use: Table/fresh market
Dry Matter: Low-medium
Yield: Good yield with early skin set
Disease Resistance: Good resistance to common scab
Storage: Good dormancy
Special Features: Low-calorie variety, early maturity and tuberisation, uniform tuber shape. Marketed as a premium early table potato.
