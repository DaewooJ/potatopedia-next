Germicopa Potato Varieties — Official Breeder Data
Source: Germicopa SAS (France, est. 1950s), www.germicopa.com
Germicopa is a French potato breeding company that has been creating new varieties since the 1950s. They provide seed to more than 70 countries worldwide.

Variety: Charlotte
Breeder: Germicopa (France)
Maturity: Medium early
Skin Color: Yellow
Flesh Color: Yellow
Tuber Shape: Long oval
Cooking Type: A (firm cooking, salad type)
Primary Use: Table, salad, retail
Dry Matter: Low-medium
Yield: Medium to high
Disease Resistance: Good general disease tolerance
Storage: Good
Special Features: One of the most popular firm-fleshed salad varieties in Europe. Excellent taste and consistent cooking quality. Widely sold in French and UK retail under its own name — one of few varieties consumers ask for by name.
